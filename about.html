Sophisticated Process

  • 01.Slice

    Everything starts with the right cut. Our chef selects premium meat and uses professional knives to slice it into perfect, even steaks. This helps to keep the meat fresh and locks in the flavor before cooking.

  • 03.Bake

    To make the meat tender, we leave it in a simple, natural marinade for a few hours. We use only coarse sea salt, fresh green rosemary, and organic spices to highlight the real taste of the steak.

  • 02.Pickled

    For the final step, we cook the steaks on a professional grill over an open fire. The high temperature quickly creates a smoky, crispy crust on the outside while keeping the inside soft, pink, and juicy.
